# 💰 Personal Finance Management Dashboard

A **Power BI dashboard** designed to help individuals track, analyze, and understand their personal finances — including income, expenses, bill statuses, and spending trends across categories and months.


## 📊 Overview

This guided Power BI project provides a comprehensive view of your financial health through a set of interactive and dynamic visualizations. The dashboard enables users to monitor spending habits, identify high-cost categories, and prepare for future expenses based on historical trends.

---

## ✨ Features

- **Total Income & Expense KPIs** — Quickly see your total income (232K) versus total expenses to be covered (57K) at a glance.
- **Month Filter (Slicer)** — Filter all visuals by one or more months (January through December) to focus on a specific time period.
- **Bill Status Chart** — A grouped bar chart showing the count of bills by **Status** (Paid vs. Unpaid) broken down by **Category** (Housing, Personal, Transportation).
- **Expense by Sub-Category (Donut Chart)** — Visualizes the proportion of total expenses across sub-categories such as:
  - TV Subscription
  - Gas
  - Electric
  - Water
  - Cleaning
  - School Loans
  - Maintenance
  - Other
- **Total Expenses vs Total Income (Gauge Chart)** — A gauge showing expenses (57K) relative to total income (232K), giving a quick sense of financial headroom.
- **Sum of Amount by Month and Category (Ribbon Chart)** — Tracks how spending across Housing, Personal, and Transportation categories shifts over time, with rank changes visualized dynamically as the selected month changes.

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

- In **January**, Gas was the highest sub-category expense.
- In **May**, Water overtook Gas as the most expensive sub-category.
- In **November**, TV Subscription became the leading expense.
- The **Ribbon Chart** clearly shows rank changes over time — for example, Housing dominated from March through April, before Transportation surged in May.
